Diversified KE

About Us

Founded in 2021 , Diversified Kenya is a grassroots queer community dedicated to creating a safe and supportive space for Lesbian, Bisexual, Queer, and Gender Non-Conforming (GNC) women in Kenya. Initially established as an advocacy group for LGBTQIA+ rights, the organization has evolved into a dynamic hub for queer youth empowerment, self-expression, and community-building. Rooted in African culture and art, Diversified Kenya fosters an environment of joy, love, and mental wellness, offering a sanctuary where LGBTQIA+ individuals can thrive despite prevalent socio-political challenges.

Our Mission

We strive to provide a safety net for queer youth in Kenya, offering economic empowerment, mental health resources, harm reduction, and community care to help them build mental fortitude and navigate their identities.

Our Vission

Empower queer youth to thrive, reclaim their stories, and build sustainable lives through resilience and community support.

Meet The Team

At Diversified Kenya our team is dedicated to creating a world in which all LBQ individuals live their lives to the fullest. Through our work in sensitization, resources and mentall health, we strive to empower members of the LBQ community and foster a more inclusive society
