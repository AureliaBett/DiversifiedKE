About Us

Welcome to Diversified Kenya, an Organization funded in 2022 to adress the unique challanges faced by LBQ persons. We are passionate about creating a safe and inclusive enviroment for all members of the community. Our mission is to educate, sensitize and empower individuals to live their lives without fear, discrimination or prejudice. We believe that everyone deserves the right to love and live openly and authentically. Join us in our mission by becoming a meber today


You deserve a circle of inclusion and influence, but it is up to you to create it.
