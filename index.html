Welcome to Diversified Kenya, an LBQ organization dedicated to empowering, educating, and sensitizing the community. Our misssion is to provide resources and to support those who need it most. We create a safe and inclusive enviroment where everyone is welcome. We believe in creating awareness about the unique challanges that the LBQ community faces, and we are commited to bridging the gap between community and society as a whole.
