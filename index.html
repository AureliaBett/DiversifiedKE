DIVERSIFIED KENYA


EDUCATE. SENSITIZE. CONNECT.

Everyone deserves a circle of inclusion and influence, but it is up to you to create it.


Welcome to Diversified Kenya, an LBQ organization dedicated to empowering, educating, and sensitizing the community. Our misssion is to provide resources and to support those who need it most.


We create a safe and inclusive enviroment where everyone is welcome. We believe in creating awareness about the unique challanges that the LBQ community faces, and we are commited to bridging the gap between community and society as a whole.


Contact us today to learn more about how we empower you.


Key Objectives

  • Economic Empowerment: Equip 200 queer youth annually with woodworking skills, financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and career development skills for economic independence.
  • Mental Wellness Through Art: Provide art therapy and creative workshops to 200 participants yearly, supporting trauma healing and personal growth.
  • Substance Abuse Reduction: Lower substance abuse rates by 30% in 3 years through peer-led sobriety support and drug-free hangouts.
  • Queer Joy & Community Resilience: Host 12 annual community events celebrating queer joy, body positivity, and mental wellness in safe spaces.
